About AYIT

Anyang Institute of Technology

Anyang Institute of Technology is located in the famous historical and cultural city－Anyang, which is one of the eight ancient capitals in China. Anyang lies in the north of Henan and borders the four provinces --- Henan，Shandong, Hebei and Shanxi, with Taihang ranges in the west, rich fields to the east, Heluo Area to the south and Beijing to the north. It has a convenient traffic with Beijing-Guangzhou railway, Beijing-Guangzhou high-speed rail, Beijing-Hong Kong-Macao expressway running from the north to the south, Changzhi-Yunzhou railway and Nanle-Changzhi expressway across the East and West. Anyang, as the hometown of Oracle-bone inscriptions, the birthplace of “Zhou Yi”, the native place of Yue Fei, and the location of Caocao’s Mausoleum, Red Flag canal and National Museum of Chinese Writing, is rich in cultural heritage.

Anyang Institute of Technology is a full-time public undergraduate college. Formerly known as Anyang University founded in 1983, the institute was upgraded to undergraduate college in May 2004 with the approval of the Ministry of Education and was renamed Anyang Institute of Technology. The School of Flight, which makes us the ninth nationally and the first provincially pilot-training university.

The institute now covers an area of 910,000㎡, with a total construction area of 643,000 ㎡, in which sports venues covers a total area of 270,000㎡. Currently there are teaching instruments and equipments worthy of 370 million yuan. It has a library collection of 1,700,000 paper books, 460,000 electronic books, 730 Chinese and foreign paper journals, and 10,000 electronic journals. Currently it has 21003 full-time undergraduates and junior college students, 18 schools and departments, and 51 undergraduate majors covering eight disciplines: engineering, science, management, economics, literature, law, arts and agronomy. The existing staff is 1015 people, including 858 full-time teachers, among which 96 are professors, 256 are associate professors and 750 teachers have Master's or higher degrees. 3 province-administered experts, 1 provincially famous teachers, 4 provincially academic technical leaders, 1 provincially science and technology innovation outstanding talent, 2 provincially outstanding youth funds gainer, 1 provincial university innovative talent, 13 provincial youth backbone teachers, 31 municipal academic technology leaders. External academicians and famous experts are more than 50 people. In recent years, Anyang Institute of Technology has been rewarded with such titles as “Provincially Civilized Unit”, “Best Demonstration Higher Education Institution in Henan in Graduates Employment Quality”, “Model University with the Best Brand Influence in Henan” and “Top 10 Exemplary University in Henan for Innovative Employment”.

The institute makes efforts to forge the following 6 major clusters: aviation, advanced equipment manufacturing, civil construction, chemical engineering, electrical and electronic information technology, biology and food and agriculture, having 6 provincial characteristic specialties, 7 provincially comprehensive reform experimental units, 2 provincial projects on reform experimental units of the model of talent cultivation, 2 provincially excellent courses, 2 provicial top-quality courses of resources sharing, 3 provincial demonstration centers of experimental teaching and 1 provincial teaching team. The institute has 3 provincially key disciplines and built 23 applied technology research platforms, 6 municipal engineering technology research centers, 8 municipally key laboratories, like " Academician Work Station of Animal Disease Prevention and Nutrition Immune ", " Anyang Institute of Technology Base of the National Key Laboratory on Cotton Biology ", " Henan Biologically Renewable Energy Equipment Engineering Technology Research Center ", " Henan High-Precision Principal Axis Engineering Laboratory ", " Machine Testing and Simulation Engineering Technology Research Center of Henan Higher Education". Kunshan Science and Technology University of Taiwan and our institute have jointly built 2 research and development centers: "Micro Precision Manufacturing" and "Environmental Engineering Technology". In recent years, more than 20 state-level scientific research projects have been approved, among which 5 stately key science and technology projects and branches of nationally key projects.
